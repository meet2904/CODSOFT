Made Like a Gun

A SHOT OF STYLE

The Hunter’s rider-first ergonomics, revised rake angle and low center of gravity have been designed to let you effortlessly maneuver the streets of the world’s coolest neighborhoods. An intuitive throttle response guarantees unprecedented agility with every flick of the wrist.

A SHOT OF TORQUE

With its shorter wheelbase, lighter weight and tighter geometry, the Hunter's frame is fitted with a super maneuverable set of wheels, a hip retro-metro aesthetic and a 350cc J-engine that packs extra muscle for every hustle.

A SHOT OF FUN

At the heart of the Hunter beats a fuel-injected, long-stroke 350cc J-engine that delivers a smoother ride, higher RPM and dollops of torque. Paired with a steady downtube spine frame and grounded suspension, the Hunter 350 gives the right amount of power and precision for revving up the city streets.

ABOUT US

Enfield: A Legacy of Motorcycling Excellence

Royal Enfield is a renowned manufacturer of modern classic motorcycles, with a rich history dating back to 1901. The company is committed to crafting iconic bikes that blend traditional design with modern technology.

Key Facts

Royal Enfield is a flagship brand with a strong presence in India, with a dealership, New Chandra Motorcycle Agency, located in Jamnagar, Gujarat.
The brand is known for its range of motorcycles, including the Bullet, Classic, INT650, Continental GT, Himalayan, and Meteor series.
Royal Enfield’s motorcycles are designed to evoke a sense of nostalgia and adventure, while also offering reliable performance and ease of handling.

Brand Philosophy

Royal Enfield’s focus is on creating motorcycles that are true to their heritage, with a emphasis on craftsmanship, quality, and attention to detail. The brand’s commitment to its legacy is reflected in its dedication to preserving traditional manufacturing techniques and materials, while also incorporating modern innovations to ensure a smooth and enjoyable riding experience.

Summary

Royal Enfield is a respected manufacturer of modern classic motorcycles, with a deep roots in history and a passion for innovation. Its dealership in Jamnagar, Gujarat, offers customers a unique opportunity to experience the brand’s iconic bikes firsthand.

Highlight Features

DIGI-ANALOG INSTRUMENT CLUSTER

A shoutout to the analogue era but perfectly in sync for modern-day riding, the Hunter features a retro-style speedometer along with a digital LCD screen that tells you everything you need to know and is compatible with the Royal Enfield Tripper Navigation System.

DUAL CHANNEL ABS

Built for unpredictable urban environments situations, the anti-lock braking system with 300mm front and 270mm rear discs bring you safely to a stop at even the shortest of distances, keeping you firmly in control.

ON-THE-GO CHARGING

The Hunter 350 comes equipped with a USB port so your devices are online while you are on the prowl.
